Graphic Design Work

This section of the digital portfolio will shows some of the Graphic Design work I have done, based of a brief Given to me

Some of the work you will see here will be either from my work I did at College or my Graphic Design course work

This is a Brainstorm of an Native American Festival, the brainstorm includes thoughts I think of when thinking about Native Americans and what their Festival will have and look like.

This Brainstorm is the starts of plans of the graphic work, once and Brainstorm is complete I then did some research and searched up some images that will be suitable and that I would migtht need to use later. With the research, I can then create a mooboard filled wth the images I have found.

This is the Moodboard I created based of the images from my search, as you can see the images are all things I think represent Native American Culture and that it would be the perfect start for potential Design Ideas.

After the MoodBoard I started to think of Potential Logos for the Main Fesstival and that Logo will be used on Posters, Banners, Brochures and other promotial content. I looked at my moodboard to see if any of the images I had found could be used when representing the logo. Out of all the Images I have two potential Images I could use for the Logo, I created them and this is how it turned out.

The end result I decided to go with the Feathered themed Logo, as it seemed to fit well with the text better than the raven did. Now that I chose A logo I can show examples of real life examples of the logo being used on t-shirts or other merchandise.

Here are the examples I created for the logo to be shown on.

As you can see the logo fits well with real life examples and will be easy to identify for the festival.


The next step was to sketch out some design, that would be logo's for some of the facilities at the festival, such as; Food stalls, Toilets, Pottery and others

These are my simple Computer based sketches I created that can then be turned into logo's for facilities around the festival.

With the Sketches done I can take all the work I have already done and put it all together in Illustrator, We need to put all the work I did on one page so that I can see all of the designs and image's I saved for Ideas

With my workspace set out I started to think on what I wanted to use for the logo's I was going to create, I based it of the sketches I created and used the Images that correlate the sketches.

These are my final designs for the logo's that will b around the festival, so it will be easy to Identify what each area of the festival is. Here I have created one based on music, pottery, the bathrooms and food.


I decided to create a poster for the festival and see how well my logo and designing will be.

I stuck with te theme of Native American Festival and found some images that would best represent it and added this to the poster, instead of plain white paper, I decided to use something that also fitswell with the Native American theme and found a sample of old fashoined paper, I lowered the opacity to make it a lighter shade so that the texts can still be seen.

I hope you have enjoyed seeing my work for this specific brief, I hope you have liked my designs and style of work.

To do this work everyday would be a dream come true, to be able to design and make people fall in love with product or company or a theme, It would mean so much to me. Thanks for taking the time to see my Graphic's work.
